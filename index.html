CrowdMe app enables one to seek and get help from a set of pre-selected friends and others who are willing to help.

One can select from their facebook friends (who have also installed CrowdMe app on their mobile devices) and add them to one's Safety Squad.

When help is desired, simply swipe on the icon in the home page. It instantly notifies your friends (on the Safety Squad) and others within a five mile radius that you need some assistance. Your friends or those willing to help you can respond to your request. The CrowdMe app guides them to your location via Google Maps. Even if you move after seeking help, the app will guide them to your current location.

  • Add some friends to your safety squad.
  • From the home screen swipe the icon to request help from friends.
  • Opt in to offer assistance to others who may need help.
