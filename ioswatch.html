Apple Watch Installation

  • Make sure you have linked your iWatch to your iPhone.
  • Click on install button from AppCenter Link.
  • Once your iPhone app is downloaded, the iWatch will automatically download to your iWatch ap
  • In case, you do not see the app on the iWatch,
    • Open the Apple Watch app (on the phone) and tap the My Watch tab
    • Scroll to Apps
    • Apps that you can install appear in the Available Apps section.
    • Tap on CrowdMe app

Using CrowdMe app from the Apple Watch

  • Login to the CrowdMe app on the iPhone.
  • Tap the CrowdMe app (icon) on the iWatch to seek help.
  • Status of help request will be displayed on iWatch
