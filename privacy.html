Privacy Policy

This app ++1 is under pre-release alpha testing. During the testing phase, user data may be retained longer than the usual 72 hours.
THIS IS A PRE-TEST RELEASE. APP MAY NOT WORK PROPERLY. PLEASE DO NOT USE FOR ANY REAL-LIFE SITUATION. THIS IS NOT A FULLY FUNCTIONAL APP. THIS IS FOR TESTING PURPOSES ONLY.
++1 app allows users to register via their Facebook account and add friends to a "Safety Squad" list. The user's account information and the list of friends on the Safety Squad list are saved on our servers. We will not share this information with any third party.
During normal operaton of the app, location information about this user is collected. This information is retained on our severs for approximately seventy two hours. During the seventy two hours, the user has the option to save this information. If they choose to save the information, only then the information is saved. Otherwise, all information is deleted after the seventy two hour retention period.
Please note: our default policy is to delete user data. If you want to save information related to help etc., please make sure you login to your account and save the information prior to the end of seventy two hours from the time the help was sought.
